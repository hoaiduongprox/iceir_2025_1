The 2nd International Conference on the Emerging Industrial Revolution (ICEIR 2025)

Nam Can Tho University, 28-29 September, 2025

The Conference on the

Industrial Revolution
(ICoTIR 2025)

is a prestigious global event that brings together researchers, scholars, and industry experts to discuss the latest advancements and challenges in the era of digital transformation. The conference provides a platform for knowledge exchange and collaboration. ICoTIR 2025 aims to foster innovation and drive impactful solutions for the evolving industrial landscape.

IMPORTANT DATES:

Submission deadline: Jul 30th 2025

Notification of acceptance: Aug 15th 2025

Camera-ready: Aug 20th 2025

Registration: Aug 30th 2025

Proceedings publication: Sep 28th 2025

Conference date: Sep 29th 2025

Journal publication: Dec 20th 2025

INTERSTED TOPICS:

The conference covers all issues in the field of Information Technology (IT), as well as interdisciplinary issues between IT and other fields of sciences, especially relative directly to the newest industrial revolution. Important, and emerging topics, as well as current scientific trends, are encouraged.

SUBMISSION GUIDELINES:

Authors are invited to submit the original and not previously published, nor currently under consideration, up to 14 pages, in English. All papers will be submitted online, in PDF format using the online submission system by Microsoft with Springer templates.

PUBLICATIONS:

All accepted papers will be published in the Conference Proceedings with an ISBN and invited for conference presentation (both online and offline). Authors are encouraged to add 30% more results for publication in the Journal of Science and Economic Development, which is indexed with an ISSN.
